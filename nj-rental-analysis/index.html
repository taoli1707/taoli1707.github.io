Methodology & caveats

Cap rate = net operating income ÷ price, where NOI = rent × 12 − vacancy loss − property tax − insurance (est. 0.4% of value) − maintenance − CapEx − management. Cash flow subtracts principal & interest at your financing assumptions. Cash-on-cash = annual cash flow ÷ (down payment + closing costs). Investment score min-max normalizes cap rate, 5-yr appreciation, vacancy (inverted), and tax rate (inverted) across all markets, then weights them by strategy — Cash flow 60/10/15/15, Balanced 40/30/15/15, Appreciation 20/55/15/10.

Market figures (median price, median rent, effective tax rate, appreciation, vacancy, rent-control status) are illustrative mid-2025 estimates embedded in the DATA array at the top of this page's script — refresh them from Zillow ZHVI/ZORI, the NJ Division of Taxation general tax-rate tables, and each municipality's rent-control ordinance before making decisions. Rent-control flags especially require local verification; New Jersey has 100+ ordinances that differ in coverage (many exempt 1–4 unit owner-occupied or post-1987 construction). This tool is for screening and education only — not financial, legal, or tax advice.
