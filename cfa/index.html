About the exam

  • Format: 180 multiple-choice questions (3 choices: A/B/C), split into two 135-minute sessions — about 90 seconds per question. No penalty for wrong answers: never leave a blank.
  • Passing: the minimum passing score is never published but is generally estimated in the low-to-mid 60% range. Scoring ≥70% consistently on practice exams is the classic readiness benchmark.
  • Calculator: only the Texas Instruments BA II Plus (or Professional) and HP 12C are allowed. Learn the BA II Plus TVM keys early — many Quant, Fixed Income, and Equity problems assume it.
  • Study volume: CFA Institute reports successful candidates average ~300 hours. Over 5 months that is ~15 hours/week.
